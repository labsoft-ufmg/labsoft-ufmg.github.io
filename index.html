LabSoft!

Software Engineering Laboratory

LabSoft focuses on the study, evaluation and definition of new technologies to overcome different dimensions of software complexity. In particular, our research investigates issues associated with the specification, construction, maintenance, evolution, reuse, and validation of software systems. In addition, LabSoft seeks practical applications of models, methods and tools to support the constant improvement of software processes, projects and products.
