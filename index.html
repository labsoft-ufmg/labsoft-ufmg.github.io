Software Engineering Laboratory

Software Engineering is a representative area within the DCC, so there have always been teachers developing related activities. The creation of the laboratory brought together professors who already worked in the area and gave students the opportunity to work in the same environment.
After its effective installation, LabSoft has been treading a path of recognition, winning national and international awards with the developed researches. Currently, LabSoft's work is focused on investigations that deal with specification, construction, maintenance, evolution, reuse and validation of software systems.
